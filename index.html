01 — Profile

Pythia is an independent advisory practice guiding private collectors and institutions through acquisitions, commissions, and long-term collection strategy.

The practice is defined by a dual foundation of curatorial rigor and global market intelligence. Francesca bridges a formal pedigree—holding an MA with Distinction from the Courtauld Institute of Art—with a decade of executive experience driving commercial strategy across the Americas and Europe.

Informed by foundational tenures at Christie’s, White Cube, and Nina Johnson, Pythia offers a highly calibrated approach to collecting. Operating seamlessly between Miami, Madrid, and Caracas, the advisory leverages a deeply entrenched, discreet network across continents.
